About Me

Hey there! I'm Denish, a Junior at Princeton University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Visual Arts and Architecture & Engineering. It's not just about the code or the canvas for me—it's the thrill of discovery, the joy of creating something new, and the conversations sparked by a fresh idea. I’m constantly inspired by the vibrant fusion of tech and design, always chasing that perfect blend. But beyond the screens and sketches, I’m also on a journey to understand the deeper impact of what we create in the tech world. As you get to know me, you'll see someone who's not just passionate about innovation, but also deeply invested in the stories and people behind it. Let’s embark on this exciting adventure together!
